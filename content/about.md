{{< section-container class="py-20 bg-gray-50" >}}
    <div class="max-w-5xl mx-auto">
        <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-center mb-12">Why Cinturon360 Exists</h2>
        <div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm p-8 md:p-10">
            <div class="prose prose-lg !max-w-none text-gray-600">
                <p>Cinturon360 is being developed to solve a common problem in managed travel: important travel decisions are often spread across disconnected booking tools, supplier channels, approval workflows, reporting processes, and manual workarounds.</p>
                <p>For TMCs, that creates unnecessary operational complexity across client onboarding, account servicing, policy management, reporting, and integration handling.</p>
                <p>For client organisations, it can mean limited visibility, inconsistent approvals, weak reporting, poor exception management, and too much dependence on fragmented processes.</p>
                <p>Cinturon360 is being built to become the operating layer above that complexity, giving TMCs and their clients a more connected way to manage travel governance, operational control, and financial visibility.</p>
